Granty Dude Streets I

The Ultimate Crime Epic

Granty Dude Streets I is an open-world, story-driven action game set in a gritty, neon-soaked reimagining of New York City. The city is on the edge of collapse crime, corruption, and chaos rule the streets, and every corner hides a new danger. NOTE: The game is not out yet. We plan to release the Pre-Beta Version on June 30, 2026. Beta and Final versions are not clear.

You play as William Ryert, a young police officer caught in a war between justice and darkness. When a powerful underground organization led by the ruthless Madison Hale rises to take control of the city, William must step into a conflict far bigger than himself. Every decision, every bullet, and every alliance shapes the fate of New York.

Explore massive open districts, survive intense firefights, engage in cinematic missions, and uncover the truth behind the Hale family's grip on the city.

Granty Dude Streets I blends explosive action, deep narrative, and a cinematic atmosphere inspired by crime-dramas and urban legends delivering a raw, emotional journey through a city worth saving or burning.
